Just after my husband I were married in 2010 he took a short-term position in Germany. From that central location we traveled (and ate!) all over Europe. We called it our last "hoorah" before the baby years and returned to The States to settle in and have that first life altering baby. One thing I learned from ordering food in many other countries is that you may think you know what you ordered and what food to expect, but chances are, you don't have any idea.
