Just after my husband I were married in 2010 he took a short-term position in Germany. From that central location, we traveled (and ate!) all over Europe. We called it our last "hoorah" before returning to the states to settle in for our first life altering baby. One thing I learned from ordering food in foreign countries is that you may think you know what you ordered and what food to expect, but chances are, you don't have any idea.
